@Title:The Dark : Signing In Waterstones, Cribbs Causeway, April 24th 1pm #The Dark : Signing In Waterstones, Cribbs Causeway, April 24th 1pm# The good, good people of Waterstones will be letting me loose in their Cribbs Causeway store on April 24th at 1pm. This is the first time I have signed in a "mainstream" book store as opposed to a specialist comic store or a convention; I'm really looking forward to introducing some non-comics fans to The Dark as well as meeting up with any comics fans who come by. There are some details of the signing up on the Waterstone's website and, of course, there is a Facebook event if you want to sign up.
